# Brand Voice Guide — TailWag Pet Wellness

## Brand Overview
TailWag is a premium pet wellness brand specializing in functional supplements for dogs. Founded by veterinarians, TailWag positions itself as the science-backed, vet-recommended choice for pet parents who treat their dogs as family members.

**Tagline:** "Because they'd do the same for you."

**Target Customer:** Millennial and Gen-Z pet parents (ages 28–42), household income $75K+, who actively research pet health and are willing to pay premium prices for quality ingredients. They shop on Amazon, read reviews carefully, and compare ingredient lists.

## Voice Characteristics

### Warm but Credible
TailWag sounds like a knowledgeable friend who happens to be a veterinarian — not a cold clinical brand, and not an overly cutesy pet brand. We're the calm, confident voice that pet parents trust.

**DO:** "Our Hip & Joint formula includes 500mg of glucosamine per chew — the same dose recommended by veterinary orthopedic specialists."

**DON'T:** "OMG your doggo is gonna LOVE these yummy chews! 🐾🐾🐾"

**DON'T:** "This pharmaceutical-grade formulation delivers optimal bioavailability of chondroitin sulfate."

### Empathetic
We understand that pet parents worry. We don't dismiss their concerns or over-promise.

**DO:** "We know it's hard to watch your dog slow down. Our joint support chews are designed to help them stay comfortable and active as they age."

**DON'T:** "Your dog will be running like a puppy again in just 2 weeks!"

### Transparent About Ingredients
Always lead with what's in the product and why. Pet parents in our segment are label-readers.

**DO:** List key active ingredients with dosages. Explain why each ingredient matters in plain language.

**DON'T:** Use vague claims like "proprietary blend" or "special formula."

## Amazon Listing Tone

### Bullet Points
- Lead with the benefit, follow with the ingredient/proof
- Keep each bullet to 1–2 sentences
- Use "Supports" language (never "treats" or "cures" — see compliance guide)
- Include dosage information where relevant

### A+ Content
- Use lifestyle imagery (dogs with families, outdoor activities)
- Include a "What's Inside" section with ingredient callouts
- Include the vet-founded story briefly
- End with a FAQ section addressing top 3 customer concerns

### Q&A Responses
- Answer within 24 hours
- Be helpful and specific — never dismissive
- Reference specific ingredients or studies when relevant
- Always recommend consulting a veterinarian for specific health concerns
- Never make claims that violate the restricted language guide

## Key Messages (Approved)
1. "Vet-formulated with clinically studied ingredients"
2. "Made in the USA in a GMP-certified facility"
3. "No artificial flavors, fillers, or preservatives"
4. "Real chicken flavor dogs actually love" (based on 94% palatability in feeding trials)
5. "Supports [specific function] — backed by science, loved by dogs"

## Words We Use
Supports, promotes, helps maintain, nourishes, wholesome, purposeful, trusted, transparent, vet-recommended, science-backed, premium, active, comfortable, thriving

## Words We Avoid
Cheap, discount, miracle, magic, guaranteed results, instant, pharmaceutical, drug, medicine, clinical trial (too medical), laboratory-tested (too cold)
