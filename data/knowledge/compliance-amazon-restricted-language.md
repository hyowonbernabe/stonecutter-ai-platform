# Amazon Restricted Language Guide — Supplements & Wellness

## Purpose
This document defines language restrictions that must be enforced across all Amazon listing content, A+ pages, Q&A responses, and marketing materials for supplement, wellness, and pet health brands. Violations can result in listing suppression, ASIN removal, or account suspension.

## Critical Rule
**Never make disease claims.** Amazon's policy (aligned with FDA/FTC guidelines) prohibits any language that claims a product can diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent a disease or medical condition.

## Prohibited Disease & Condition Names

The following terms must NEVER appear in product listings, A+ content, bullet points, titles, or Q&A responses:

### Human Health
- Cancer, tumor, carcinoma
- Diabetes, diabetic, blood sugar disease
- Heart disease, cardiovascular disease, heart attack
- Alzheimer's, dementia
- Depression, anxiety disorder, clinical depression
- Arthritis, rheumatoid arthritis
- Osteoporosis
- High blood pressure, hypertension
- High cholesterol, hypercholesterolemia
- IBS, Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis
- Insomnia (as a diagnosed condition)
- ADHD, ADD
- Autism
- Parkinson's disease
- Multiple sclerosis
- Lupus
- Fibromyalgia
- Eczema, psoriasis, dermatitis (as conditions to treat)
- UTI, urinary tract infection
- Kidney disease, liver disease

### Pet Health
- Hip dysplasia
- Canine cancer
- Feline leukemia
- Parvovirus
- Kennel cough
- Mange
- Seizures, epilepsy

## Prohibited Action Verbs

These verbs imply therapeutic claims and must not be used in connection with health outcomes:

- **Treats** / treat / treatment
- **Cures** / cure
- **Prevents** / prevent / prevention
- **Heals** / heal / healing
- **Eliminates** (when referring to symptoms or conditions)
- **Fights** (when referring to disease)
- **Kills** (bacteria, viruses, pathogens — unless EPA-registered)
- **Diagnoses** / diagnose
- **Reverses** (a condition)
- **Repairs** (tissue, organs — unless cosmetic context)

## Approved Alternative Language

Instead of disease claims, use structure/function claims:

| DON'T SAY | DO SAY |
|-----------|--------|
| "Treats joint pain" | "Supports joint comfort and flexibility" |
| "Cures acne" | "Promotes clear, healthy-looking skin" |
| "Prevents heart disease" | "Supports cardiovascular health" |
| "Lowers blood sugar" | "Supports healthy blood sugar levels already in the normal range" |
| "Fights depression" | "Supports a positive mood" |
| "Heals gut issues" | "Supports digestive wellness" |
| "Treats dog anxiety" | "Helps promote calm behavior in dogs" |
| "Cures hip dysplasia" | "Supports hip and joint mobility" |
| "Prevents hair loss" | "Supports healthy hair growth" |
| "Boosts immunity to prevent illness" | "Supports immune system function" |

## Required Disclaimers

All supplement listings MUST include (in A+ content or product description):

> *These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.*

## Review & Enforcement

- All new listing copy must be scanned against this document before publishing
- Quarterly audit of all active listings for compliance
- Any flagged language must be corrected within 24 hours
- Account managers are responsible for compliance on their client accounts
- When in doubt, escalate to the Account Director before publishing
